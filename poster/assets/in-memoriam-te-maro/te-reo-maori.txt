Hei whakamaharatanga ki a Te Maro
He tohunga mō ngā tipu,
te tāmata me te ngaki māra hoki
he mea pupuhi ki te manawa
e ngā kaihōpara i arahina e Kāpene Kuki
i te 8 o Oketopa i te tau 1769,
te rā tuatahi o tā rātau whakaekenga,
e 500 tau i muri iho i te taunga tata mai
o te waka Horouta
e haramai ana ki Aotearoa i Hawaiki.
